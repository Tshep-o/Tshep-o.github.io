Support Page

  • Bring an audio file that is on your phone into Repetita by pressing the add icon in the navigation bar on the tracks view
  • To add a snippet, press the add button that appears next all added tracks
    Use the sliders to set the beginning and end of your snipper, then hit the large add button
    in the center to add the snippet. To view the snippet, clicking on a track will
    present a view with all the snippets for that particular track.
  • To "drill" a single snippet, click on the green drill button that appears on the snippet
  • To "drill" a all the snippets of a track, click on the orange drill button that appears at the top of the snippets view
  • To set the repeat count, click on the settings button which appears at the top of the snippets view

    The repeat count determines how many times a snippet is played before stopping playback when drilling a single snippet
    and how many times each snippet should be played when drilling all the snippets of a track.
